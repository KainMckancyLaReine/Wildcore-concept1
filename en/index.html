Why Wildcore

This weekend isn't about performing.
It's about experiencing.

For us, trail running isn't about speed or competition. It's about being outdoors, moving in nature, and noticing what happens once you step away from the rush of everyday life.

In the Sierra Calderona we run by feel. No strict schedule, no focus on times or performance. We stop where it's beautiful, look out over the valley, take a photo, or simply stand still in nature for a moment. Walking is always allowed, anywhere.

This weekend is for anyone with a reasonable base fitness and a healthy dose of curiosity — whether you've been running for years or have never set foot on a trail.

Your home base

Villa El Pinar, near Casinos

During this weekend you won't stay in a hotel, but in an atmospheric, detached villa that feels like a home away from home — in the quiet urbanisation of El Pinar, right next to the authentic village of Casinos.

The villa sits amid greenery with views over the mountains of the Sierra Calderona. After a day on the trails you unwind by the pool, on the terrace or in the garden. Inside, it's all about a warm, homely atmosphere: coffee in the morning, stories after the trail run, and healthy, wholesome meals without any rush.

  • Panoramic mountain views
  • Shared living space
  • Large garden to unwind in
  • Breakfast, lunch & dinner included
  • Room for everyone, no rush
About your host

Maurizio Salazar

I live at the foot of the Sierra Calderona for much of the year. By profession I'm a sports and shiatsu therapist, and I've been fascinated by movement, health and the power of nature my entire life.

Through my work I see every day how important it is to step out of your daily routine and make time for yourself. Nature has always been a place for me to recharge — and that sense of rest and freedom is what I love sharing with others.

That's why I organise small-scale trail running weekends. Not a performance-driven sports trip, but an accessible experience where movement, nature, relaxation and connection take centre stage. I'd love to take you into the mountains.

— Maurizio

Open to anyone aged 18 or over with a reasonable base level of fitness. You are responsible for assessing your own physical condition and health. The group is kept deliberately small, so there's room for personal attention and everyone can move at their own pace, running or walking.

Accommodation at the villa, breakfast, lunch, 2 or 3 shared dinners, airport transfer from Valencia Airport, trail transfers and guidance on every run.
